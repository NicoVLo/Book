Artist Statement

I am an inexperienced artist. I do not look to provide purpose when creating and my art does not specifically reflect myself. My process of creating is driven by curiosity, meaning I want to explore a variety of mediums without being limited to any particular subject matter. At my current stage, art is for my own self enjoyment, although sometimes it communicates something about me. I feel incapable compared to my peers, however my feelings of amazement at their abilities are far more intense. My goal is to create work that forces me to learn something new to create it.
